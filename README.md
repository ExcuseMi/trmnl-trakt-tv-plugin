### Description
Track your Trakt.tv activity right on your TRMNL device.<br /><br />
<strong>Available categories</strong><br />
· <strong>Continue Watching</strong> — paused shows &amp; movies<br />
· <strong>Recently Watched</strong> — your watch history<br />
· <strong>Upcoming</strong> — next episodes &amp; releases<br />
· <strong>Recommended</strong> — personalized picks from Trakt<br />
· <strong>Watchlist</strong> — your saved items<br />
· <strong>Collection</strong> — your collected media<br /><br />
<strong>Requires Trakt.tv OAuth</strong>
